Our Cuisines

We offer three popular cuisines for our subscribers. Each cuisine features a rotating menu of freshly prepared meals so you never get bored!

Arabic Cuisine

Our Arabic cuisine menu features traditional Gulf and Middle Eastern dishes made with authentic spices and fresh ingredients. These are the meals you grew up with, cooked with love and care.

Example Meals:

  • Chicken Machboos with basmati rice
  • Grilled Chicken with Arabic salad
  • Hummus and grilled kebab platter
  • Lamb kebab with flatbread and pickles
  • Mixed grill with tahini sauce

Asian Cuisine

Our Asian cuisine brings the best flavours from across Asia. From stir-fried noodles to sushi rolls, enjoy a wide variety of popular Asian dishes delivered fresh.

Example Meals:

  • Vegetable stir-fry noodles
  • Chicken fried rice
  • Sushi rolls (salmon and tuna)
  • Teriyaki chicken with steamed rice
  • Thai green curry with jasmine rice

American Cuisine

Enjoy classic American comfort food delivered to your door. Our American menu includes hearty burgers, crispy fries, and other favourite dishes that everyone loves.

Example Meals:

  • Classic cheeseburger with fries
  • Grilled chicken sandwich with coleslaw
  • Spaghetti and meatballs
  • BBQ ribs with baked potato
  • Caesar salad with grilled chicken
