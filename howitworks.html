How It Works

Getting started with SavorySphere is easy. Just follow these simple steps and you will have fresh meals delivered to your door in Bahrain.

Steps to Subscribe

Choose Your Cuisine

Pick from our three available cuisines: Arabic, Asian, or American. Each cuisine has a rotating menu so you always get something new.

Select a Subscription Plan

Choose the subscription duration that suits you best: 1 Month (25 BHD), 3 Months (70 BHD), 6 Months (130 BHD), or 1 Year (240 BHD). Longer plans save you more money.

Enter Your Delivery Address

Provide your full Bahrain delivery address including house/building number, road, block, area, and city. This is required so we know exactly where to deliver your meals.

Choose Payment Method

Select your preferred payment method. We accept debit/credit cards, BenefitPay, and cash on delivery.

Receive Your Meals

Once your subscription is confirmed, we will start delivering fresh meals to your address. Sit back, relax, and enjoy your food!
